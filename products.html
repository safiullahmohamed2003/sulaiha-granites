Finishes We Offer

Pick the right surface

Polished

Glossy & reflective

Our most popular finish — brings out the stone's natural colour and pattern. Best for counters and indoor flooring.

Honed

Smooth, matte look

A flat, non-reflective surface that hides scratches well — good for high-traffic flooring.

Flamed

Textured & slip-resistant

Rougher surface created with heat treatment — ideal for outdoor steps, driveways and pool surrounds.
